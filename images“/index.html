Who we are?

A service or after-sales service defines an assistance and support service provided by a brand for anyone who has purchased or consumed its products. The purpose of after-sales service is to ensure that consumers are satisfied with their purchase or order, and to provide assistance in the event of a problem or breakdown. Indeed, the purchase of a new product always comes with the risk that it does not work properly or does not quite meet our expectations. It is in these cases that it is imperative to contact the after-sales services, which are managed by customer and technical advisors who can solve any type of problem and complaint.

If your product needs a repair, if you want to exchange an item for a product that suits you better or if you simply want to request a refund following a purchase, the first reflex you must have is to contact the after-sales service of the store or brand where you made your purchase. The advisors will be able to inform you on several subjects such as your rights of withdrawal and warranty, or the most suitable solutions to your problem. The after-sales service is also generally competent to manage and resolve all complaints in order to avoid disputes.

All major retailers set up a service service, reachable by different means: by phone, online and by mail. But beware, the largest brands have millions of customers and often provide specific contacts for each service to respond more effectively to the requests of their subscribers. It is therefore easy to get lost between customer service numbers, after-sales service numbers, emergency numbers, mediator numbers, numbers from abroad, etc., and to know their opening hours. Similarly, some steps with after-sales service require contact by email or mail to keep written proof, and again, it is not always easy to find up-to-date contact information. This is where we come in, allowing you to find the right contacts in a few clicks or connecting you with the right interlocutors in a few minutes!
